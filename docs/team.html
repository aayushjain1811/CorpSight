Satyajeet Yadav

Founder & Managing Partner

Satyajeet Yadav is the Founder and Managing Partner of CorpSight Advisors LLP, bringing over a decade of deep expertise in corporate law, regulatory compliance, and business advisory. He has advised a diverse portfolio of clients — from early-stage startups to established corporates — across the full spectrum of entity incorporation, ongoing compliance management, SEBI frameworks, FEMA regulations, and FDI structuring. Known for his precision and pragmatic counsel, Satyajeet combines a thorough understanding of India's evolving legal landscape with a results-driven approach that empowers businesses to grow with confidence and compliance.

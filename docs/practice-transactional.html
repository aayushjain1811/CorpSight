Our Services

01

Legal Due Diligence

Comprehensive review of corporate documents, contracts, litigation, intellectual property, regulatory compliance, employment matters, and title to assets.

02

Financial & Tax Due Diligence Coordination

Coordinating financial, tax, and accounting due diligence with specialist firms and synthesising findings into a unified risk assessment.

03

Transaction Structuring

Advising on optimal deal structures ‚Äî asset purchase vs share purchase, merger schemes, restructuring, earn-outs, and deferred consideration mechanisms.

04

Share Purchase & Business Transfer Agreements

Drafting, reviewing, and negotiating SPAs, BTAs, asset purchase agreements, and related transaction documentation.

Investment Documentation

Regulatory Approvals for Transactions

Preparation of vendor-side due diligence reports and data room management to facilitate efficient and confident investor review.

Post-Transaction Integration Advisory

Support on post-closing integration tasks including entity restructuring, employment transfers, contract novations, and compliance harmonisation.

What We Do

Our Services

01

Company & LLP Incorporation

Formation of private limited companies, public limited companies, LLPs, OPCs, and Section 8 entities under the Companies Act, 2013 and LLP Act, 2008.

02

Regulatory Registrations

Obtaining GST registration, PAN/TAN, MSME registration, Shop & Establishment licences, and other mandatory registrations based on your industry and location.

03

Shareholding & Capital Structuring

Advice on equity and debt capital structuring, authorised share capital, preference share issuance, and founder shareholding arrangements.

04

Corporate Governance Setup

Drafting of Articles of Association, Shareholders' Agreements, Board governance policies, and related foundational documents.

05

Annual ROC Compliance

Preparation and filing of annual returns (MGT-7), financial statements (AOC-4), board meeting documentation, and statutory registers.

06

Registered Office & Statutory Filings

Maintenance of registered office address, changes to company particulars, and all event-based ROC filings throughout the year.

07

Conversion & Restructuring

Conversion of proprietorships, partnerships, or LLPs into companies; conversion between company types; business restructuring and reorganisation.

08

Compliance Health Audits

Periodic audits of your statutory compliance status, identification of gaps, and actionable remediation plans to avoid penalties.
